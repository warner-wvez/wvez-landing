The backstory

Why did I build this?

Started as ▾
A sneaker kid's instinct
I grew up reselling sneakers all through middle and high school. I always knew how often profitable flips would go live, but eBay's built-in notifications were too slow to catch any of them. I wanted something faster.
Became ▾
Buy signals, not checkmarks
My first version just monitored keywords every few minutes. It worked, but I was still spending too much time checking if the "deal" was truly a deal. Skimming descriptions, titles, accounts. Constant notifications. Lost time AND lost money. So I pivoted to finding the perfect products, ones I knew would sell quickly and turn profit after fees. The goal became a "buy signal," not a "go check."
Result ▾
Zero-thought flip pipeline
A fully automated system that scans 200+ listings per run across four product categories, filters out junk, pulls real sold comps, and only alerts me when the math says buy. Every alert is pre-validated: net profit, ROI, fees, shipping, all calculated before it hits my phone.
Biggest lesson ▾
The research was the real build
The hardest part wasn't the code. It was the research. Days of manually finding products that would guarantee profit after taxes, fees, and shipping. Even more time went into coding the junk title filter. Countless executions watching what crossed the table, figuring out what didn't belong, and building the regex that catches broken screens, accessories, and parts listings before they waste your time.

Step by step

How it works

01Authenticate + Load Keywords3 nodes▾
One click starts everything. The system logs into eBay's API and loads a curated list of product keywords with price ranges, each one pre-validated for consistent resale profit.
02Scan + Loop Keywords3 nodes▾
For each keyword, the system pulls the 50 most recently listed items from eBay (used, US-only, fixed price). It loops through every keyword with a 2-second pause between each to stay under API limits.
03Filter Pipeline4 nodes▾
Four layers of filtering strip out everything that isn't a real, buyable product. Flattening, price range checks, condition verification, and a 60+ term regex engine that catches broken screens, replacement parts, accessories, and dozens of false positives.
04Identify, Price & Validate4 nodes▾
A deterministic parser extracts the exact model from every title. Each model gets priced against up to 60 real sold comps, then true net profit is calculated after marketplace fees, payment processing, and shipping. Only listings clearing minimum profit and ROI thresholds survive. Passing deals get formatted into a structured alert.
05Send Buy Signal1 node▾
Surviving deals are sent as a clean Telegram alert with everything needed to act: product name, buy price, expected profit, ROI percentage, and a direct link to the listing. One tap to buy.
