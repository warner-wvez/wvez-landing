The backstory

Why did I build this?

Started as ▾
A quick favor for a friend
My friend was trying to grow his Instagram account and I asked him, "hey, would you be interested in a system that can pull engagement data from any account you choose?" After the quick yes, I got to work. Shoutout to @sheancreates.
Became ▾
A full content intelligence engine
What started as simple data pulls evolved into a system that uses Google Gemini to watch the first 10 seconds of every video, transcribe the hook, and categorize each post into a "content pillar", giving him a complete picture of what types of content actually drive engagement.
Result ▾
1,000+ videos analyzed automatically
The workflow now pulls the most recent 50 videos from any creator of his choosing. He can see the post date, URL, likes, comments, captions, hashtags, length, views, engagement score, AI-assigned content pillar, and the literal first sentence of each video. He now knows EXACTLY what works, for himself and his competitors.
Biggest lesson ▾
AI sees what humans scroll past
The biggest unlock was using Gemini to actually watch and categorize the videos. Humans scroll past 50 reels and remember nothing, the system watches all 50 (or more), transcribes every hook, and spots patterns you'd never catch manually. The first 10 seconds tell the whole story.

Step by step

How it works

01 Trigger 1 node ▾
You click one button. That's it, the whole system starts running and does everything else automatically.
02 Scrape Creator Content 1 node ▾
Goes to Instagram and pulls the most recent 50 posts from any creator you choose, their captions, views, likes, comments, video URLs, everything.
03 Standardize 1 node ▾
Cleans and organizes the raw data, calculates engagement scores, formats dates, and strips out the noise so every post is comparable.
04 Batch Processing 1 node ▾
Processes posts in small groups of 5 to stay within rate limits and keep the system reliable, no crashing, no skipped posts.
05 Download Media 1 node ▾
Downloads the actual video or image file for each post so AI can watch and analyze the content directly.
06 AI Video Analysis 1 node ▾
Google Gemini watches the first 10 seconds of every video. It transcribes the hook word-for-word and assigns a content category based on what the video is actually about.
07 Output to Google Sheets 2 nodes ▾
Everything lands in a clean Google Sheet, date, creator, URL, views, likes, engagement score, pillar, and the literal first sentence. Ready to sort and compare.
